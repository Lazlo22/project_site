Burj Khalifa

World tallest building

General Information

The Burj Khalifa, known as the Burj Dubai prior to its inauguration in 2010, is a skyscraper in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. With a total height of 829.8 m, the Burj Khalifa has been the tallest structure and building in the world since its topping out in late 2008. The building was opened in 2010 as part of a new development called Downtown Dubai. The building broke numerous height records, including its designation as the tallest building in the world.

World records

  • Tallest existing structure: 829.8 m
  • Building with most floors: 163 previously
  • World's largest light and sound show staged on a single building
  • World's highest New Year display of fireworks
  • World's highest nightclub: 144th floor
  • World's longest travel distance elevators: 504 m
  • World's highest restaurant: 122nd floor at 442 m
